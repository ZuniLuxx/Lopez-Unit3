Types of trickshotting in Black Ops 1 and Black Ops 2.

Insta Swap

Is an action in the game where you switch weapons faster than usual using any equipment you can cancel.How to? Press:Y+LB+Y

Nac Swap

Is an action in the game where you switch weopons faster than usual by shotting your shotgun.How to? Shoot shotgun+X+Y

Can Swap

Is an action in the game where you switch weopons faster than usual by Picking up a weopon from the ground. How to? Find a gun on the ground+hold X+Y. When you switch to your weopon it does a cool animation.

Pistol Switch

Is the normal action where you just switch weopns. How to? Press Y.
